Foundations and Reflections

What Surprised Me the Most About Human Skills Learning

Throughout my time in foundations, I have discovered new things about myself that have stuck with me. Exploring my ego has been eye-opening. Engaging in the mental exercises has brought about a positive shift within me. I have always thought of myself as being a person free from negativity, with empathy being one of my strengths. However, the listening and reflection exercises revealed a new and indescribable experience.

The Roles: Empathy, Values, and Self-Awareness

Understanding the importance of self-awareness and self-regulation in programming has been a significant lesson for me. Recognizing and managing my emotions, thoughts, and reactions throughout these past weeks has been essential in getting myself through this. Empathy, in particular, stands out as a crucial attribute. The ability to empathize and connect with others on an emotional level greatly enhances collaboration and productivity in any work environment. I admit i have not done very much online interaction with other students this is something i am starting to get a better handle on and i will not shy from the task away when needed. These human skills have been unexpectedly enlightening in an industry where I least expected it.

The Most Challenging Parts for Me

I was surprised by the emphasis on cultural awareness. I have never considered myself to have any real type of culture at all. However, I now realize I have just never looked deep into that side of myself to realize that I actually do have a culture of my own. Through research and reflection, I discovered a deeper connection to my own cultural roots. My family, I found, is my cultural identity. Realizing this has been a new experience for me. I think I have just misinterpreted the word all these years.
