My goals are simple, to obtain the necessary qualifications for a career in technology or something similar, allowing me to secure a employment with a stable income to provide for my wife and children.


My strengths lie in innovation and a willingness to continually learn. I consider myself clever enough to manage a wide range of problems in my day-to-day life. Working with mechanical, electrical, and digital machinery has been the primary focus of my entire career.
Over 20 years ago, I became the owner and operator of a business, which taught me a great deal about workplace challenges and how to overcome them. Dealing with employees can be difficult at times, but I always practiced patience and understanding, which I found to be very effective in resolving issues. I believe that staying composed and listening carefully can easily resolve most of the problems faced.
Throughout many years in the industry of digital and offset printing, my brother and I accomplished tasks that at times seemed impossible. But we almost always managed to produce the product on time and with high quality. If we were ever unable to produce on time I always found honesty the best way to handle any clients questions.


My limitations are more physical than mental. I sustained an injury about 7 years ago, resulting in compound fractures in my ankle, making it challenging for me to move around continuously. Due to this, I believe that remote or hybrid style employment would be the best fit for me.
One of my challenges is explaining something until I have full confidence in the subject. At the moment, I find it difficult to explain many of the things I have learned so far and also in the previous months leading up to this course. However, I learn something new every day, so I anticipate not having a problem explaining the subjects I am learning through this course in the near future.


My main source of assistance is my brother, who has been in the tech industry for a long time. I would say he was learning javascript when it first came out and has not stopped learning, He does show a very high level of Growth Mindset. I know I can also reach out to others on the Discord page anytime , this is not really my comfort zone but i will adjust.


In facing pressure, I will try my best to collaborating with fellow learners and facilitators. Should the demands become excessive, I will prioritize self-care by taking a break. My goal is to achieve success, and I am determined not to let challenges escalate beyond my control.

I have certain family commitments, but fortunately, my wife understands and supports my goals. She is assisting me as I go through this course, and I am grateful for her kindness and understanding. With her help, I am hoping to be able to effectively manage and fulfill all of my commitments without too much difficulty.
